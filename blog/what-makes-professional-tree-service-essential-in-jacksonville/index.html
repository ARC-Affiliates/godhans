When is the best time to schedule tree trimming in Jacksonville?

The optimal time for tree trimming in Jacksonville depends on species type, but late winter through early spring generally provides ideal conditions for most pruning work. Dormant season trimming minimizes stress on trees, reduces disease transmission risk, and allows arborists to clearly see branch structure without foliage obstruction. According to Godhans, Jacksonville homeowners should schedule oak trimming between December and February to avoid oak wilt disease spread, while pine species tolerate pruning throughout the year. Hurricane preparation trimming should occur in April or May, before storm season begins but after winter weather passes. Dead or hazardous branches require immediate removal regardless of season.
